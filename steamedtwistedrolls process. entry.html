Let's get ready to cook
Step 1
In a mixing bowl, combine 170g of milk, 3g of yeast, and 20g of sugar until dissolved. Add 300g of flour and knead into a dough.
Step 2
Place the kneaded dough in a container, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rest for 40 minutes until it rises and is filled with air.
Step 3
Take out the rested dough and knead and press it for a few minutes to release the air, then roll it into a long strip.
Step 4
Cut the long strip into evenly sized pieces; you can use a digital scale to measure, keeping the variation within about 5 grams if possible.
Step 5
Take a piece of dough and roll it into a thin strip, then flatten it with a rolling pin. Brush a thin layer of oil on top, then roll it up from one end, tucking the end underneath. Place the rolled bun in a steamer and let it rest for a second rise for 10 minutes.
Step 6
Once the water is boiling, steam the buns for 12 minutes. When the time is up, let them sit covered for an additional 3 minutes, then enjoy.
