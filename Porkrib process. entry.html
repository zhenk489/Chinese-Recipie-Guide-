Let's get ready to cook
Step 1
Rinse the ribs thoroughly, place them in cold water, and add green onions, ginger, and cooking wine. Blanch, then remove and set aside.
Step 2
Heat oil in a pan, add two spoonfuls of rock sugar, and cook over low heat until it melts and turns a reddish-brown caramel. Once it reaches this stage, it's ready.
Step 3
Add the ribs to the pan and stir-fry. Pour in half a spoon of dark soy sauce, two spoons of light soy sauce, three spoons of vinegar, a few slices of ginger, and some green onion pieces. Add boiling water until the ribs are fully submerged.
Step 4
Cover the pan and simmer on high heat for about 30 minutes. Remove the aromatics, then cook on high heat to reduce the sauce. Serve the ribs on a plate, and if available, sprinkle with some sesame seeds.
