What you need before start the recipe
  • Pan or iron wok
  • Seasoning Products:

    Soy sauce, pepper, rice vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, oyster sauce, starch.

  • Pickling session

    Marinate the diced chicken breasts in soy sauce and oyster sauce, scrunch them up and marinate for five minutes, add corn starch and corn oil to lock the chicken in moisture and marinate for ten minutes or more.

Gather your ingredients
Kung Pao Chicken is a classic Chinese stir-fry dish made with diced chicken, peanuts, and vegetables, all tossed in a flavorful, spicy-sweet sauce. This Sichuan-style recipe combines the heat of dried chili peppers with a tangy soy-based sauce.
