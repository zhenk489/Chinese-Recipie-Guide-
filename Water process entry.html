Let's get ready to cook
Step 1
Remove the skin from the pork belly and chop it into minced meat. Finely chop the water chestnuts and set them aside. Soak the goji berries in water and set them aside.
Step 2
Add an appropriate amount of salt, chicken bouillon, and a bit of pepper to the minced pork belly for seasoning. Mix in one egg white, the chopped water chestnuts, and a small amount of cornstarch, and stir until well combined.
Step 3
Rub some cooking oil on your hands, take an appropriate amount of minced meat, and squeeze it through the thumb and forefinger to form round meatballs.
Step 4
Add mineral water to the pot and bring it to a boil. Then, reduce to low heat and maintain a gentle simmer. Place the formed meatballs into the water, add a slice of ginger, and simmer for 6 minutes.
Step 5
Finally, add the goji berries, salt, and light soy sauce for seasoning. Sprinkle with chopped green onions for garnish before serving.
