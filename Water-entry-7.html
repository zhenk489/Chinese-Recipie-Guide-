马蹄肉丸汤
Water Chestnut Meatball Soup
Water Chestnut Meatball Soup is a light and flavorful Chinese dish featuring meatballs made from minced pork or beef mixed with chopped water chestnuts, adding a satisfying crunch and natural sweetness. The meatballs are simmered in a clear broth infused with ginger and scallions.
From Guangdong
Southern part of China
